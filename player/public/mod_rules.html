The Rules

How to play Space Invaders

You have won the game when you have claimed the most resources at the end of the game.
So you are thinking, how do I win that magnificent awesome prize?
Write a program (bot) that is the best at invading space!
Our matchmaker will continually be pitting the latest bots against each other to determine the greatest Space Invader.
Whoever has the highest score in the last game, wins!

Contact one of the organizers to have an account created for you.
Then improve upon the example bot of your choice and upload it.
Download the example bot in the language of your choice:
Java macro/Java micro
C# macro/C# micro
Python macro/Python micro

Good luck!
Let's start

This year's challenge has 2 parts: macro and micro.
Macro is about strategy, determining where you want to send your ufo's, which planets to conquer, and buying new ufo's.
Micro is about tactics, shoot down your enemies opponents and live to fight another day.
You can even compete in teams!
One person can handle the macro while the other focuses on the micro.
However, it is up to you to figure out how to divide the awesome prize!

Ethics in Space
We trust that any code you upload is not malicious.
Do not write to disk or devices in your code.
Do not connect to the internet
Do not try to starve your opponents for cpu time or memory space
Failure to comply will result in disqualification
